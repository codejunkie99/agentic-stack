## Purpose
Present the case team's interim findings to the client steering committee — what we've found, what it means, what still needs resolution, and the direction of final recommendations.

## Contents
1. Situation Recap (1 slide — the question and our approach)
2. Workstream Findings (3-5 slides per workstream)
3. Cross-Workstream Synthesis (2-3 slides — emerging themes)
4. Preliminary Recommendations (directional, not final)
5. Risks and Open Questions (what could change our conclusions)
6. Next Steps (refined workplan to final deliverable)

## Team Structure: Coordinated
- **framework-lead** leads and coordinates:
  - **case-analyst** — Per-workstream findings
  - **deck-builder** — Slide structure and titles
  - **delivery-lead** — Next steps and workplan
- framework-lead runs internal coherence review before director escalation

## Review Panel (independent, parallel)
- **partner-strategy** — Strategic direction and client-readiness
- **partner-analytics** — Analytical rigor
- **principal-delivery** — Timeline and delivery credibility

## Output Format
Ghost deck (slide titles + exhibit outlines) for user review before full content build.
Final output: full slide outline in markdown.

## Quality Gates
- Every finding slide title carries the insight (not the topic)
- Preliminary recommendations are directional and supported by findings
- Open questions section is honest about what we don't know yet
- Next steps have owners and dates
