## Purpose
Produce a structured situation assessment that aligns the case team on the client context, the core problem, initial hypotheses, and analytical approach for the engagement.

## Contents
1. Client Overview (business model, financials, market position)
2. Problem Statement (specific business challenge being addressed)
3. Diagnostic Questions (the key questions to answer)
4. Initial Hypotheses (directional claims to prove or disprove)
5. Analytical Approach (workstreams, methods, data requirements)
6. Constraints and Boundaries (what is in/out of scope)

## Team Structure: Flat
Orchestrator spawns specialists directly:
- **framework-lead** — Issue tree and hypothesis structure
- **case-analyst** — Client context and market benchmarks
- **delivery-lead** — Workstream and workplan structure

## Review
- **partner-strategy** — Business logic and problem framing
- **principal-delivery** — Workplan feasibility

## Output Format
Markdown document following pyramid principle structure.
Max 5 pages. Partner-readable in 10 minutes.

## Quality Gates
- Problem statement is specific and falsifiable
- Hypotheses are directional (not "it depends")
- Workplan has explicit owners and deadlines
- Scope boundaries are stated
